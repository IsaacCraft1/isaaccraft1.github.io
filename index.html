How to Play Table Tennis

Core Rules of the Game

  • The Service: The ball must bounce once on your side, clear the net, and bounce on the opponent's side.
  • Scoring Points: Games are played up to 11 points.
  • Alternating Service: Service alternates every 2 points.

Why Table Tennis is Beneficial

Physical & Mental Advantages

  • Cardiovascular Health: Spikes your heart rate and burns calories efficiently.
  • Hand-Eye Coordination: Sharpens control and spatial tracking capabilities.
